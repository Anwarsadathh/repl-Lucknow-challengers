About REPL Sports

Initiative of Rudrabhishek Trust for Sports and Education (RTSE) - A Not-for-Profit Organization

REPL Sports has the singular agenda of bringing 'sports' at the centre stage of everyone's lifestyle. We want to inspire and facilitate people's shift from drawing-room confined recreation activities to real classic sports tradition in-ground, in-stadium and in-club activities.

Sports, where there is physical activity as well as real interaction with other people which gives us physical fitness, emotional fulfilment and fosters a sense of comradery. In sports, you win something every time, even if you lose a game.

Meet The REPL Lucknow Challengers

"Shaping Future Stars"

REPL Sports proudly steps into the Lucknow Premier League with the launch of its dynamic new franchise — REPL Lucknow Challengers. Built on passion, performance, and purpose, the team represents a bold commitment to nurturing cricketing talent and shaping the future stars of the game.

The franchise blends professional excellence with grassroots development, providing a competitive platform for emerging players to showcase their skills, gain exposure, and grow under a high-performance sporting ecosystem.

With Lucknow at its heart, the Challengers embody resilience, ambition, and the fighting spirit of the city. As the lights shine bright and a new chapter begins, REPL Lucknow Challengers is ready to take on the league with confidence, discipline, and an unyielding desire to win.
